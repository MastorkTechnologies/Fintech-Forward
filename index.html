Meet the Guest of Honour

Mr. Dipesh Shah

Executive Director, IFSCA

We are privileged to have our esteemed guest of honor, a Senior Business Leader with over 22 years of unparalleled experience in the financial services sector. With a proven track record in setting up large-scale projects, our guest has been instrumental in advancing International Business Development, International Financial Services, Strategic Planning, Project Development, Corporate Law, and policy matters across both Indian and international markets.

A notable highlight of our guest's illustrious career includes successfully establishing and operationalising India's first Smart City and International Financial Services Centre (IFSC) at GIFT City. Their visionary leadership and strategic acumen have not only transformed the landscape of the financial services sector but also set new benchmarks for innovation and excellence.

Event Venue

Constitution Club of India

A club that comprises of the elite Indian Constituent Assembly, The Constitution Club of India, was inaugurated by Sarvapalli Radhakrishnan in February 1947 for members of the Indian Constituent Assembly. In present days the Constitution Club has effectively emerged as an enviable forum providing a platform for interaction and networking.
The Constitution Club of India in New Delhi offers modern conference halls, dining and banquet options, a library, comfortable accommodations, sports facilities, lounge areas, and a business centre, making it a hub for legislative and governmental activities.
